Christmas Eve

It is our tradition to offer free entrance on Christmas Eve as we love to give a gift to the city of Aalborg and to send off many happy guests. What is even more exciting is that this year marks our 10th anniversary.

Come visit the zoo, meet Santa and greet the reindeer before he goes on gift delivery. We invite everyone to our Christmas party, young and old.

Feeling generous?

During this day you will have the opportunity to save and support your favourite animals.
