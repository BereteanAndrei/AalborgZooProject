Our 10th Christmas Eve

It is our tradition to offer free entrance on Christmas Eve as we love to give a gift to the city of Aalborg and to send off many happy guests. What is even more exciting is that this year marks our 10th anniversary.

Come visit the zoo, meet Santa and greet the reindeer before he goes on gift delivery. We invite everyone to our Christmas party, young and old.

Feeling generous?

During this day you will have the opportunity to save and support your favourite animals.

The Black Mamba Project

What do we do?

The Black Mamba anti-poaching unit’s main objective is the security of the reserve and the protection of wildlife.

26 brave women create shelters for elephants and other types of wildlife, each of them patrols the area for around 20km/day and they arrest and scare off poachers, while destroying snares and traps.

They want their communities to understand that the benefits are greater through wildlife conservation rather than poaching, addressing the social and moral decay.
